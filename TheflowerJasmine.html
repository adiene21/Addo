The Flower Jasmine

The Flower Jasmine has a hypnotic aura, with ebony as her color cream
The first day I smell her in the garden, her scent occupy my dream
The moon takes over as she glows in the dark; my mind follows
As soon as the sun rises, she becomes invisible as I turn into shadows.

As she disappears out of my captive sight
The thoughts of her run wild in the cold night
Would fate have it that I could see her again?
Among all the flowers, she is the unique elixir of my pain.

How am I going to find her now? With my Heart!
Thank to Da Vinci, he has painted a picture of her in his art
Mona LiFa! Sweet is the Flower Jasmine the day I find
You, the river of love flow; as its gates reopens up again in my mind.

She is mature, educated and beautiful
Her nature is modest, sincere and respectful.
The Flower Jasmine, her scent in me is nothing ephemeral
My undying love for the Flower Jasmine rest eternal!

By A.M.D.
